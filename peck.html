How to play

You're running a chicken farm! Try to stay profitable by managing your grain orders and selling eggs or chickens. Make sure your chickens aren't hungry or sad!

  • Hunger decreases happiness.
  • Happiness affects resell price.
  • Drop grain to feed chickens.
  • Eggs are laid at 6am if a chicken is happy enough.
