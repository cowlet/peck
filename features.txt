+ Chickens have happiness counters
- Change pointer between grain or ruffle
- Ruffle increases happiness
- Happiness decreases over time

+ Chickens have satiation/hunger counter
+ Eating grain increases satiation
+ Satiation decreases over time

+ Have a day/night cycle
+ Change colours/drawing for day/night

- Can require certain amount of food over day/night
- Can lay eggs

- Egg schedule dependent on happiness
